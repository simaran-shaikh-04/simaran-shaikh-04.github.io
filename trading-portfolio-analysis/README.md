## 📌 Project Overview

Analysed a real client trading portfolio of ₹5,00,000 
with ₹3,00,000 already deployed across 3 stocks and 
₹2,00,000 cash awaiting deployment. Conducted 
fundamental analysis of all stocks using 15 key 
financial ratios and recommended optimal deployment 
strategy for the remaining cash.

> **This was a live client portfolio with real money 
> — not a simulated exercise.**

## 🔍 Analysis Framework

### Ratios Used (15 metrics per stock)

**Valuation:**
- Price to Earning (P/E) vs Industry P/E
- Price to Book Value

**Profitability:**
- ROCE, ROE, EPS
- Average ROE 3 Years

**Growth:**
- Sales Growth (Annual)
- Sales Growth 3 Years
- Profit Growth

**Risk:**
- Debt to Equity
- Interest Coverage Ratio
- Promoter Holding %

**Price Momentum:**
- Return over 3 Months
- Return over 6 Months
- Down from 52W High

## 🚨 Key Findings

### Motilal Oswal — HOLD
- Trading 34% above Industry P/E despite 
  22% fall from peak
- Profit degrowth of -25.3% — costs rising 
  faster than revenue
- Sales growing but profitability deteriorating

### Sagility India — AVERAGE DOWN 
- Trading 18% BELOW Industry P/E
- Profit growth of +74% — exceptional
- Virtually debt free (D/E: 0.11)
- Classic price-fundamentals divergence

### HDFC Bank — HOLD
- Slowest growth in portfolio (Sales: 3.64%)
- Worst price momentum (-23.8% over 6M)
- Safe but no compelling reason to add

### Colgate India — NEW POSITION 
- ROCE 105%, ROE 81.2% — world class quality
- Virtually debt free (D/E: 0.04)
- Defensive FMCG hedge for volatile portfolio
- Sector diversification from Finance/IT/Banking
